What's your flavorite was created to make your life easier. You may have several ingredients, but what should you make? Let us help you find the perfect recipe. See the steps below to know how to find your flavorite recipe!

Step 1

On the "Recipes" page choose the ingredients you would like to use in your recipe. You can choose as many as you want.

Step 2

Based on your ingredients choice, you will see recipes that you can cook using them! "Flavorite" the ones you like the most. Then go to "Your Flavorites" page to see them.

Step 3

If you don't feel like cooking at this time, no worries. Just send your flavorites to your email, then you can cook whenever your want! Easy, huh? So, let's go.
